Scrap Vessel Trading Globally


Global Trading acquires Offshore Platforms, Rigs, Drilling Vessels, Barges, Dredgers, Tankers, Bulkers and other vessels for recycling or demolition.

Vessel demolition involves the dismantling of vessels, as both a source of spare parts, which can be sold for reuse or for the extraction of raw materials, primarily scrap metal steel plate recycling.

Ship dismantling involves the retired ships being stripped of their machinery, barring the value-adding materials which are rightfully reused for new ships or other applications.

The primary and the most common way to dismantle a vessel is by breaking it apart into several different parts before breaking them further. On some of the biggest ship breaking yards in the world such as Alang in India, the process of dismantling starts by beaching the ship on the shore.

At Global Trading all vessels will be considered for purchase and are uniquely positioned to take delivery where the vessel is currently located worldwide creating a seamless transition between us and the seller.
